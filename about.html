ABOUT MALAYSIA STREET FOOD

Malaysia’s food streets are a vibrant reflection of the country’s rich cultural diversity, offering an exciting mix of Malay, Chinese, Indian, and other ethnic cuisines.

OUR MISSION, VISION, VALUE

MISSION

Our mission is to showcase Malaysia’s diverse street food culture.

VISION

Our vision is to be the go-to online destination for discovering Malaysia’s food streets.

VALUE

Our value is authenticity, community, cultural appreciation, and passion for great food.
